Outfit Contest

Welcome to Undaunted Insomniac's first Class Outfit Contest! With all the motifs flying around, we thought now would be a great time for everyone to show off their ESO drip.
The rules are simple, and the prizes are BIG! The goal is to submit an outfit that you think best represents your characters class (Templar, Dragonknight, Necromancer, Warden, Sorceror, or Nightblade). One winner from each class will be chosen and EACH winner will receive 1 MILLION gold! To enter, simply post screenshot(s) of your character to #class-contest-submissions. Entries close 11:59pm PDT on April 16th, and the winners will be announced on April 17th!
