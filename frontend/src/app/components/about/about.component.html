About

Undaunted Insomniacs is a community focused on bettering skills and improving at ESO. We feature a guild and a discord of the same name, and take pride and passion in helping others become more experienced, more comfortable, and pull higher numbers. As long as you are willing to learn, there is information here to help you.

We have a friendly community of players experienced with different play styles and classes. Within the Undaunted Insomniacs discord, you can ask questions and get one-on-one help about your build and how to change it.

We’d love to have you along for the ride – whether you’re an old player looking to participate in some vet content, or a new player hoping to improve, you’re always welcome with Undaunted Insomniacs.
